## 1. Who We Are

AOWATT Global Materials operates this website at aowatt.com.au. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information collected through the site.

## 2. What We Collect

This is a static marketing website. We do **not** run inquiry forms, accounts, or any feature that asks you to submit personal data. The site does not set cookies for personalisation or advertising.

Aggregate, non-personal traffic data is collected by:

- **Cloudflare** — for DNS, CDN, and aggregated visitor analytics (no cookies).
- **Vercel Web Analytics** — for aggregated page-view and custom-event analytics (no cookies).

We do not sell, share, or transfer this data for advertising purposes.

## 3. Direct Contact

When you contact us via email, phone, or WhatsApp using the links on the Contact page, the message and any information you choose to include are handled by the respective communication provider (your email client, telephone carrier, or WhatsApp) under their own terms. We use the contents of your message solely to respond to your enquiry.

## 4. Third-Party Processors

The third parties named in §2 process limited data on our behalf:

| Processor | Purpose | Data |
|---|---|---|
| Cloudflare | DNS, CDN, traffic analytics | IP address, request metadata |
| Vercel | Hosting, web analytics | IP address, page path, event names |

Each provider operates under its own privacy policy. We recommend you review them directly.

## 5. International Processing

Because we work with international buyers and use global infrastructure providers, technical data and direct enquiry records may be processed or stored outside your country of residence, including in Australia, the United States, and other jurisdictions where our service providers operate.

We use this information only for website operation, security, analytics, and responding to your business enquiry.

## 6. Your Rights

You may contact us at [info@aowatt.com.au](mailto:info@aowatt.com.au) to request information about how we handle data related to your enquiries with us, or to ask us to delete records of past correspondence.

## 7. No Sensitive Information

Please do not send passwords, payment card details, personal identity documents, or other sensitive personal information through email, WhatsApp, or any website link unless we specifically request it through an agreed secure process.
